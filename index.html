Projects


GetFit


New users sign up by creating a profile and can then login to start using the app. The premise of the app is having users enter their location to find gyms closest to them. I chose to develop this because I enjoy working out and since this was my last project, I really wanted to build something I'm passionate about and enjoy doing.



Eventalist


The purpose is to have users come and view events in categories such as art, music, and theater currently being held in the New York City area. The app has the ability to have users subscribe to events through providing their email based on the category they are interested in, events are updated daily and users are sent reminders of these updates.
